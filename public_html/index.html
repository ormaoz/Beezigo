About

Beezigo is a location based app which allows you to know how busy a popular place is in real time. With Beezigo you can decide if a place is too busy and thus go somewhere else, have the app snooze you when it has reached the level of busyness of your choice or offer alternatives nearby. Currently Beezigo supports only main attractions in NYC.

Beezigo was developed by the Media Innovation Lab (miLAB) of the Sammy Ofer School of Communication in IDC Herzliya. All photos are courteously contributed by the Time Out NYC which holds all rights.

Students: Gili Amram, Gal Ben-Shalom, Nimrod Klinger, Or Maoz, Ran Romano, Yully Sessler, Adi Shoel & Gali Swery.

Technology Mentor: Eran Ilani, Adam Rosental, Ido Orlovski, Oz Radiano

Design: Hamutzim Studio

Mentor: Noa Morag
